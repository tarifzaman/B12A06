About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. We believe we can start, one tree at a time. Our goal is to plant 100 million trees, empowering individuals like you to make a tangible difference. Together, we can create habitats for wildlife, and combat global warming.

  • Restoration of natural habitats
  • Combating climate change
  • Support for local communities
